Dataset: HW_III.xlsx (Given)  —  43 sales orders · Jan 2021 – Dec 2022  |  Fields: OrderDate, Region, Rep, Item, Units, Unit Cost, Total  |  Tool: Vega-Lite v5, five interactive views assembled into one multiview specification
I.

Donut Chart — Total Sales by Representative

Proportion of revenue attributable to each sales representative. Hover a slice to inspect the exact figure.

II.

Streamgraph — Monthly Sales by Product

Symmetrically stacked area (stack: center) tracing each product's revenue contribution across the two-year window. Hover for exact month and amount.

III.

Trellis Histogram — Units Sold Distribution per Item

Faceted frequency histograms, one panel per product category. A dashed vertical rule marks the mean unit volume for each panel.

IV.

Interactive Heatmap — Revenue by Representative × Product

Click any row to isolate a sales representative; remaining rows fade. Colour intensity encodes total revenue. Hover a cell for a full breakdown.

V.

Linked Brush — Monthly Totals → Per-Representative Breakdown

Drag a selection across the upper bar chart to define a date window; the lower chart immediately refilters to show only the representatives active in that period.

§

Multiview — All Five Charts in a Single Vega-Lite Specification

The five views above concatenated via vconcat into one self-contained Vega-Lite specification, as required by the assignment.
